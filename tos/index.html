The AOB x LOS Scrim Hub exists to organize and track competitive scrims between participating teams. By using it, you agree to the following:

General Rules

  • Rosters submitted must accurately reflect who actually played in a given scrim
  • Scores and results must be reported honestly and promptly
  • No-shows, deliberate stalling, or repeated scheduling abuse may result in scrim privileges being suspended

ELO & Ranking Disputes

ELO or ranking adjustments are calculated based on reported match outcomes. If you believe a result or ELO adjustment is incorrect:

  • Raise the dispute through the designated Discord channel (e.g. #patch-notes or a staff-facing ticket) within 48 hours of the match in question

  • Staff decisions on ELO disputes are final. We aim to be fair and consistent, but ELO is an internal community ranking system, not a competitive league with formal arbitration

We reserve the right to void, adjust, or roll back ELO changes found to be the result of manipulation, collusion, or a reporting error.
